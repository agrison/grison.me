First of all, I wish you all a Happy new year, and the very best for you and all the people you care about throughout the whole year 2013. Staying healthy being the most important fore sure.

I have some good resolutions that I would like to keep during the forthcoming year, we will see if I can keep them up.

Sport, sport, sport

During the last 2 years, I‘ve gained something that I didn‘t want named fat. What I want is to lose that damn weight. This is the year, and so I‘m getting back to:

  • Badminton, at least once a week
  • Running, at least once a week. I would like to do both Luxembourg and Brussels Half-Marathon
  • Fitness, at least once a week for a starter (can be the same day as running), then when I‘m feeling better keep on with Olivier Lafay's book
  • Football, from time to time

I wish I will be able to keep with these objectives, the most important being to lose at least 15 kilograms, no matter what.

Developers, developers, developers!

Everyday I check HackerNews at least 10 times, same for reddit and on other blogs I follow. And what I can see is people having plenty of good ideas (that for sure I don't have) bust most importantly creating and delivering something out of these ideas. When I say everyday, I mean everyday!

I still want to improve my skills, and I want to create things, deliver them, and want people to use them. From time to time, it‘s hard to see everyday something brilliantly new and well executed, and thinking I‘m not part of anything Ã¼bber-cool

I know I‘ve got some talent, so this year I would like to deliver some things, it may be JSSG or even Peculiar but for sure this year I will do so.

On top of my list:

  • Updating this website more than often.
  • JSSG.
  • Peculiar.
  • Kree.
  • A new one that could be a spin-off of Peculiar based on the same technologies when I will be done with it, in order to keep an eye on my diet evolution, BMI and stuff like that. Peculiar was created as a clone of an excel sheet, and since I have another one excel sheet to manage this, a new web app like Peculiar makes sense, at least to me :).
  • A Python / Django / Bootstrap / YouNameTheAwesomeNewTechnology webapp to manage restaurants that could be made open source, and that my in-laws parents could use to promote and manage their own restaurant.

Piano

I was at a friend‘s house for new year evening, and some time after the well-known count from ten, my friend played some piano. The fact is he doesn‘t know the whole music theory stuff and he mostly learned by watching videos on youtube and using Synthesia. Still it was impressive, nice and all to hear him play some tunes like Tetris and other cool ones.

I have a piano since 2 to 3 years, and I don‘t know how to play any music for more than 10 seconds. I would really like to improve on that. I hope I will do so.
